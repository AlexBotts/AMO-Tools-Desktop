Compute the quantity of air required by a specific single acting or a double acting piston cylinder compressor. The design specs of the compressor are entered in the calculator and the quantity of air needed is generated.

Cylinder Diameter

Inner diameter of cylinder.

Cylinder Stroke

Distance that the piston can travel inside a cylinder.

Piston Rod Diameter

Diameter of the piston rod (required only in case of a double acting cylinder).

Cycles Per Minute

Number of cycles (1 cycle is a combination of 2 strokes of a piston) by the crankshaft in a minute.
