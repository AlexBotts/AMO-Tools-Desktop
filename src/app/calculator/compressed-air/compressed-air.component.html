Compressed Air Calculators

Leak Loss Estimator - Bag Method

Estimate compressed air consumption using the bag method.

Pneumatic Valve Flow Factor

There are two calculators in this section. The first one can be used for finding flow rate for a pipe with flow coefficient Cv=1. The second one is used for calculating Cv when the flow rate is known.

Pneumatic Air Requirement

Compute the quantity of air required by a specific single acting or a double acting piston cylinder compressor. The design specs of the compressor are entered in the calculator and the quantity of air needed is generated.

Receiver Tank

Use general method to calculate receiver tank size.

Pipe Sizing

Find the pipe diameter, when we know the volumetric flow velocity, pressure and design velocity are known.

Velocity in the Piping

Find the velocity of compressed air through all the different piping involved in the system.

System - Capacity

Find the total air quantity that the compressed air system can contain at any instant of time, including the air in the pipes and receivers. The total capacity of the compressed air system can be obtained by adding the volumes of pipes and receivers.

Operation Cost Calculations

Find the cost of operation of the compressor in both fully and partially load instances.
