General
General method is used when the user needs to order a receiver tank to meet the compressed air requirements.
Air Demand
Amount of air needed, which comes out of the receiver tank.
Allowable Pressure Drop
The pressure drop inside the receiver tank within the tolerable limits.
Atmospheric Pressure
General Value is 14.7 psi. In case the receiver tank is at higher altitude location, the respective atmospheric pressure at that location can be given.
