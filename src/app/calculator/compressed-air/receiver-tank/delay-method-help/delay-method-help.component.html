This method calculates the size of the tank when air is removed from the tank for a few seconds before which the compressor can start reacting and pump more air. So an initial pressure might be created, which should be avoided.

Distance to Compressor Room

Distance of the event, i.e removing the air, from the compressor room .

Speed of Air

Flow rate of air from the tank.

Air Demand

Quantity of air that is required for the event.

Allowable Pressure Drop

Limit on the permissible pressure drop.

Atmospheric Pressure

General Value is 14.7 psi. In case the receiver tank is at higher altitude location, the respective atmospheric pressure at that location can be given.
