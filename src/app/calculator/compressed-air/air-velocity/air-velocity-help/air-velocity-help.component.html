Velocity in the Piping Help
This calculator helps in finding the velocity of compressed air through all the different piping involved in the system.
Air Flow
Volumetric flow rate of the air in the compresser system, referenced to the compressor inlet conditions.
Pipe Pressure
Pressure of air flowing through the pipe.
Atmospheric Pressure
General value is 14.7 psi. In case the receiver tank is at higher altitude location, the respective atmospheric pressure at that location can be given.
