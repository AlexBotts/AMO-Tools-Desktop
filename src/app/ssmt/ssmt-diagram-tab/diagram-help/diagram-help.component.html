Steam Diagram Help
The Diagram screen shows a diagram of the steam equipment in the assessment.
Information is available for the steam properties of the pipes and equipment by hovering over a component, a section of pipe, or an arrow.
Hover over an arrow to see the steam properties at that specific point.
Hover over pipes to see an overview of the steam properties within that pipe section.
Hover over equipment for an overview of the characteristics and inputs to that equipment.
Clicking on specific pieces of equipment will bring up more detailed information in the Results tab.
The meanings for each color of the pipe are outlined below.
  • Feedwater
  • Blowdown
  • Vents
  • Condensate
  • Return Condensate and Make-up Water
  • High Pressure Header
  • Medium Pressure Header
  • Low Pressure Header
