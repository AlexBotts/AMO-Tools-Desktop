Header Help
Enter measured data to calculate your system's annual savings potential.
Number Of Headers
Choose the number of headers from the options (1, 2, or 3). With two or three headers notice that you must specify the highest and lowest pressure header.
Pressure
Pressure of the high pressure header. Pressure of the medium pressure header. Pressure of the low pressure header.
Process Steam Usage
Mass flow of steam used by processes. Includes any header steam leaks.
Condensate Recovery Rate
Percent of process steam used that is returned as condensate.
Condensate Return Temperature
Average temperature of combined condensate returning.
Flash Condensate Return
Select YES if the model uses a flash to reduce the pressure of the combined condensate before joining the makeup water stream.
Flash Condensate Into Header
Select YES if the model uses a flash tank to reduce the pressure of the combined condensate, sending the flash steam to another header.
Heat Loss
Estimation of header heat loss, as a percent of energy flow.
Desuperheat Steam Into Highest Pressure Medium Pressure Header
Models the addition of feedwater into the pressure reduce valve.
Desuperheat Temperature
The target desuperheating temperature for the PRV outlet. Must be below outlet temperature without desuperheating to have an effect.
