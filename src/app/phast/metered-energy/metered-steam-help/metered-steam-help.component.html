Metered Energy Help
Enter data about the energy of processing heating equipment based on metered steam use. This allows the user to compare PHAST calculations to the meter data of the equipment. This method calculates total energy used by a furnace by multiplying metered steam energy used by the measurement period (hours/days/weeks).
Heat Content of Steam
Total heat contained in steam. This can be calculated with the steam properties tool, based on the temperature and pressure of the steam.
Steam Flow During Data Collection
Metered flow rate of steam, as measured during the collection period.
Steam Collection Time
Time period over which steam meter data was collected.
Electicity Used During Calcuation
Metered electricity used by auxiliary equipment during collection period.
Collection Time (Aux. Equipment)
Time period over which auxiliary electricity meter data was collected.
