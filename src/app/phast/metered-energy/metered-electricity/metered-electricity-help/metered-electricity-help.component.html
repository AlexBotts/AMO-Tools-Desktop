Metered Energy Help

Enter data about the energy of processing heating equipment based on metered fuel use. This allows the user to compare PHAST calculations to the meter data of the equipment. This method calculates total energy used by a furnace by multiplying metered fuel energy used by the measurement period (hours/days/weeks).
