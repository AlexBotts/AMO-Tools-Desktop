Total Outside Surface Area (ft2)

Total area of the exposed parts such as shafts, structural components of the furnace, part of the charge being heated (if applicable), burner or radiant tubes, material handling system (conveyors, belts) etc. Estimate based on actual measurements or use furnace drawings.

Average Surface Temperature (℉)

Average temperature for the exposed areas. The average should be calculated based on actual measurement at several locations for the exposed parts or areas. The actual temperature can vary over a wide range and care should be taken to use weighted average. Use infra-red radiation temperature measurement instrument (with proper emissivity correction) whenever possible.

Ambient Temperature (℉)

Temperature of the area outside the furnace where the exposed areas are located. In most cases this is the ambient temperature in the area where the furnace and exposed areas are located.

Surface Emissivity (Typical - 0.9)

Emissivity of the surface from heat is dissipated or lost. This can be in the range of 07 to 0.9 for commonly encountered surfaces but the user should use appropriate value for the specific case.
