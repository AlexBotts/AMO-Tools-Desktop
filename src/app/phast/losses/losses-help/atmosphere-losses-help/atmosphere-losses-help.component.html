Atmosphere Gas

Select gas from the drop down list. The gas properties cell below the drop down list will be filled in if the gas is selected from the list. If it is different from the gas listed, then select Other.

Specific heat of gas (Btu/(SCF-(℉)))

Give average value of the specific heat for the temperature range of the gas inlet temperature and outlet temperature.

Initial temperature (℉)

Inlet temperature (Celsius) of the process atmosphere as it enters the heating equipment or reaction chamber. Use temperature recorder/indicator reading or actual measurement.

Final temperature (℉)

Outlet temperature (Celsius) of the process atmosphere as it leaves the heating equipment or reaction chamber. Use temperature recorder/indicator reading or actual measurement.

Flow Rate (SCF/hr)

Atmosphere flow rate in terms of standard volume flow rate. Note that it is at standard conditions and actual flow values given by a flow meter should be corrected for the pressure-temperature condition at the measurement point. Use readings from a flow meter.
