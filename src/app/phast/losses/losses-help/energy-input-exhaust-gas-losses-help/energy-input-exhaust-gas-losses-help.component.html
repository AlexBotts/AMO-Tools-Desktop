Exhaust Gas Help

Enter exhaust gas data

Total Heat Input

Give value of total heat input to the heating system (furnace/oven) from all sources of heat supplied

Units: Btu/hr or kJ/hr
Minimum Value: 1
Maximum Value: 3

Electrical Power Input

Measured power input from the power meter.

Units: Kw
Minimum Value: 0

Excess Air

Excess air used for combustion. This can be estimated by measuring fuel and air supply or use 20% if it is not known, For some infrared ovens it could 100% or higher.

Units: %
Minimum Value: 0

Combustion Air Temperature

Combustion air temperature at the burner.

Units: oF or oC
Minimum Value Allowed: 0

Exhaust Gas Temperature

Exhaust gas temperature from the oven or furnace.

Units: oF or oC
Minimum Value Allowed: 0
