Furnace Draft Pressure (Inch W.C.)

Furnace pressure measured at the level of the opening. It is measured by using an inclined tube manometer or a digital gage. Units of measurement is in terms of inch water column. Hot gases leak out when the pressure is positive. Cold air leaks into the furnace when pressure is negative.

Opening Area (ft2)

This is value of open area from where hot gases are leaking out.

Temperature of Gases Leaking (℉)

Temperature of gases leaking out of the opening. In most cases it is safe and accurate enough to measure and use furnace (or zone) temperature for this value.

Specific Density of Flue Gases (Air = 1.0)

Specific density of gases at standard temperature - pressure (STP) measured relative to air using air specific density as 1.0 which translates as 0.0763 lb./scf.

Ambient Temperature (℉)

Ambient temperature at the location where opening is present. Normally this is room temperature in the area where furnace is located.
