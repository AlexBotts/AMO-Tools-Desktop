Designed Energy Help
Enter data about the design of processing heating equipment. This allows the user to compare PHAST calculations to the design of the equipment. This method calculates total energy used by a furnace by multiplying total rated capacity of burners (Btu/hr) used in different heat zones with annual operating hours, percentage rated capacity used and duty cycle.
Fuel Type
Fuel used in heat zone.
Total Burner Rated Capacity
Total rated capacity of all burners in zone.
Percent of Rated Capacity Used
% of capacity in use by furnace.
Percent of Operating Hours
Percent of operating hours that the zone is running.
